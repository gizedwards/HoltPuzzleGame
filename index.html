Captain Holt's Island Riddle

These are the instructions:

There are 12 people on an island, they all weight the same except one.

One of the islanders is either heavier or lighter.

You have to find out who it is, you only have a seesaw...which you can use 3 times.

Click and drag the islanders on the seesaw whichever way you wish.

Click the 'Weigh' button to find out what side is heavier or lighter.

Use the drop down menu under the seesaw to select your choices. Then click the 'Choose Wisely' button.

You will have 3 attempts to weigh, and 1 chance to choose the character.

These are the controls

Click 'Weigh' after you have place islanders onto the seesaw.

An alert will tell you which side of the seesaw is heavier.

'New Game' will refresh the page, it will also give you a new weight and islander
